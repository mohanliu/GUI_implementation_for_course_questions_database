For the $M_i = \frac{\sigma_{\text{TS}}}{\rho}$ selection criterion, which material has the maximum materials index?

\begin{multicols}{2}
	\begin{choices}
	\choice Flexible Polymer Foam
	\choice Wrought Magnesium Alloys
	\choice Ceramic Foam
	\correctchoice CRPF
	\choice Softwood
	\choice Concrete
	\choice Tungsten Alloys
	\choice Gold
	\end{choices}
\end{multicols}


\begin{solution}

Here, we note that if we draw a line parallel to the selection criteria, up and two the left, we have a higher performance index. This is shown in green in the solution graph, where we find the CRPF is the best-performing material for this criterion.

\end{solution}

\begin{rubric}

1 pt for CRPF, 1/2 pt for anything on the left side of the line, 0 pts for gold, concrete, or ceramic foam.

\end{rubric}
