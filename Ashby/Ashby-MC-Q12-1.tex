%Solution----------------------------------
\begin{solution}

If we are minimizing $M = E/k^2$ we want small values of $E$ and large values of $k$. This is Quadrant 2.

You could do the entire problem, which would give you a slope of 1/2. Minimizing this value would give you an answer in Quadrant 2. 5-10 materials in this quadrant are better than materials in either Quadrant 1 or 3

\end{solution}
